Our Heritage & Story

Our Journey

Tending the Flame Since 1995

The story of Shama Brothers BBQ is a narrative written in smoke, embers, and unyielding passion. It began over three decades ago in the historic heart of Hyderabad, Sindh, when our founder, Aamir Shama, set up a modest single charcoal grill with nothing but a hand-ground spice blend and a dream.

What set Shama apart from day one was a refusal to take shortcuts. In an era of gas grills and pre-packaged masalas, the Shama family chose the hard way: slow-grilling over real local coal, grinding spices fresh each dawn, and treating every street guest like royalty.

Today, Shama Brothers has evolved into a landmark destination. Guided by three generations of the Shama family, our kitchen remains true to those founding principles, sending the same mouthwatering aromas of authentic desi BBQ into the Hyderabadi night sky.

1995
The Roadside Ember
Aamir Shama opens a single-grill seekh kebab stall in Saddar, Hyderabad, using a secret 12-spice family recipe.
2005
First Brick & Mortar Diner
Driven by a loyal following, Shama Brothers moves into its first dining room, expanding the menu to include legendary mutton platters.
2015
BBQ Catering Across Sindh
Expanding outside the restaurant walls, Shama Brothers launches street event catering, feeding massive family gatherings and festivals.
Today
A Hyderabadi Landmark
Now operating a two-story culinary destination, serving over 50,000 satisfied guests annually with the same commitment to live fire.
